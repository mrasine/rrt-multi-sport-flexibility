Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: March 23, 2026

Our Commitment

Rhythmic Ribbon of Texas is committed to making our website accessible to all visitors. We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA.

What We Do

Our website includes semantic HTML with proper heading hierarchy, alt text for images, keyboard navigation support, skip-to-content links, ARIA landmarks and labels, visible focus indicators, and sufficient color contrast. We continuously work to identify and fix accessibility issues as they arise.

Feedback

Report an Accessibility Issue

If you encounter any barriers while using our website, please let us know. We welcome your feedback and will work to address issues promptly.
