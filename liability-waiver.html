Liability Waiver & Assumption of Risk

Last Updated: March 23, 2026

Important Legal Information

This document contains important legal terms. By participating in any flexibility training services offered by Rhythmic Ribbon of Texas, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Liability Waiver and Assumption of Risk agreement.

1. Acknowledgment of Risks

Flexibility training and rhythmic gymnastics activities involve inherent risks, hazards, and dangers that cannot be eliminated. By participating in training with Rhythmic Ribbon of Texas, you voluntarily assume all risks of injury, harm, or loss that may occur during or after training sessions.

These risks include but are not limited to:

  • Muscle strains, pulls, and tears
  • Ligament sprains and tears
  • Joint injuries including sprains and dislocations
  • Bone fractures and stress fractures
  • Overextension and hyperextension injuries
  • Back and spine injuries
  • Neck injuries
  • Bruises, cuts, and abrasions
  • Falls and impact injuries
  • Equipment-related injuries
  • Heat-related illnesses
  • Cardiovascular stress
  • Neurological injuries
  • Paralysis or permanent disability
  • Death

2. Physical Condition Representation

You represent and warrant that:

  • You are physically fit and have no existing medical condition that would prevent safe participation in flexibility training
  • You have received and will continue to receive regular medical check-ups as recommended by your physician
  • You will immediately disclose any injury, illness, or physical limitation to your trainer before or during any training session
  • You will not participate in training while under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or any substance that could impair your judgment or physical ability
  • You understand that flexibility training requires progressive conditioning and you will not force your body beyond its current capabilities

3. Medical Clearance

You acknowledge that you should consult with a qualified medical professional before beginning any flexibility training program. This is especially important if you:

  • Have a history of joint, muscle, or bone injuries
  • Have been diagnosed with osteoporosis or other bone conditions
  • Have cardiovascular conditions
  • Have hypertension or low blood pressure
  • Are pregnant or recently gave birth
  • Have any chronic medical conditions
  • Are taking medications that may affect physical performance

4. Proper Technique and Supervision

You agree to:

  • Follow all instructions provided by your trainer regarding proper technique and form
  • Ask questions if you do not understand any instruction
  • Immediately stop training if you experience pain, dizziness, nausea, or any unusual symptoms
  • Not attempt advanced techniques without proper preparation and supervision
  • Use equipment only as instructed

To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, you hereby:

  • WAIVE, RELEASE, AND DISCHARGE Rhythmic Ribbon of Texas, its owner(s), coaches, employees, agents, and representatives from any and all claims, demands, or causes of action arising from or related to any injury, harm, or loss you may suffer during or as a result of training
  • COVENANT NOT TO SUE Rhythmic Ribbon of Texas or any of its associated parties for any injury or damages arising from your participation in training activities
  • ACKNOWLEDGE that this waiver covers injuries caused in whole or in part by the negligence of Rhythmic Ribbon of Texas, its employees, or agents, except where such negligence amounts to gross negligence or intentional misconduct

6. Indemnification

You agree to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Rhythmic Ribbon of Texas and its owner(s), coaches, employees, and agents from and against any and all claims, damages, losses, costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorney fees) arising from:

  • Your negligent or wrongful conduct during training
  • Your violation of these terms or any instructions provided by your trainer
  • Any injury to yourself or others caused by your actions during training

7. For Parents/Guardians of Minors

If the participant is under 18 years of age, the parent or legal guardian must:

  • Read and agree to all terms of this waiver on behalf of the minor participant
  • Understand that the minor will be participating in physical activities with inherent risks
  • Accept responsibility for monitoring the minor's physical condition before and during training
  • Agree to indemnify and hold harmless Rhythmic Ribbon of Texas as outlined in Section 6

By inquiring about or scheduling group training services, you represent and warrant that:

  • You accept responsibility for ensuring a safe training environment

9. Insurance

You acknowledge that Rhythmic Ribbon of Texas does not carry liability insurance for participants. You are encouraged to maintain your own personal liability and health insurance coverage.

10. Severability
